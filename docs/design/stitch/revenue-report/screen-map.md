# Screen Map

## Purpose

Reference the admin revenue report for PayOS revenue, issued credit, and reconciliation.

## Main Sections

- Date range and day/month controls.
- Metric cards for paid amount, issued credit, successful transactions, and reconciliation needs.
- Revenue chart.
- Trend and verification summary cards.
- Reconciliation table.
- Ledger safety note.

## Implementation Notes

- Revenue values are placeholders and do not define accounting formulas.
- CSV export requires backend/API support before implementation.
- Keep the ledger warning visible when implementing revenue reconciliation.

## Phase Alignment

Aligned with Phase 8 slice: admin revenue and credit reporting.

## Deferred Items To Avoid

- Refund automation.
- Other payment providers.
- Subscription billing.
