# Form Automation Workflow Design Notes

## Review Result

Accepted as a Phase 5 design reference.

## Visual Review

- The page uses the approved dashboard shell and compact B2B style.
- The flow is represented as a clear stepper from analyze to confirmation.
- The rule editor, preview, and confirmation areas are visible in one workflow.
- No visible payment UI, AI auto-submit, or official Google OAuth/API production connection appears in the screenshot.

## Safety And Scope Notes

- Preview before submission must remain mandatory.
- Generated response count must stay between 1 and 5 per action.
- `UsageLogs` and `SubmissionLogs` mentions are implementation reminders, not new contract definitions.
- Sample questions, entry IDs, and submission IDs are design placeholders.
